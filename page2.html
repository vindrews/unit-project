Half-Life's Sequels

Half-Life is a series with 15 games in it, and yet there are only two that follow proper linear progression. Half-Life 1 and Half-Life 2 are about as linear as one can get with Valve games. After the release of Half-Life 2, Valve released Half-Life 2: Episode 1, which was a continuation of the story of Half-Life two. They then released a bunch of other games with titles like this, some of them following the progression of the story and some of them just being little side quests. A running joke in the Valve fandom is that the company can never make a third game. Half-Life, Portal, and Team Fortress seem to be stuck with only a sequel for the forseeable future.
